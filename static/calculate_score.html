Instead of using GWAS data from our database, you are welcome to upload your own GWAS data. Your file must* be tab separated (either .tsv or .txt), and have the following columns: Study ID?, Trait?, Rsid?, Chromosome?, Position?, Risk Allele?, Odds Ratio?, and P-value?. Optional column headers that will be included if present are: Citation? and Reported Trait?. All other columns in the file will be ignored. If more than one odds ratio exists for a dbSNP reference (rs) number, the odd ratio and corresponding risk allele with the most significant p-value will be used.
