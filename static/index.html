What is the PRS Knowledge Base?


The purpose of the PRS Knowledge Base is to allow researchers to quickly calculate polygenic risk scores for various traits based on published genome-wide association studies (GWAS).

The PRS Knowledge Base uses data downloaded and curated from the NHGRI-EBI GWAS Catalog and is regularly updated to include the lastest studies, SNPs, and associations. The PRS Knowledge Base currently supports nearly 800 traits and over 2000 GWA studies.
