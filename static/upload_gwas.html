How to Upload GWAS

All GWAS uploads are manually analyzed to ensure that they meet our quality standards. Check the following criteria before submitting the form below to ensure your study gets added to our database.

Criteria

  1. The study is not already included in the PRS Knowledge Base (check using the "Cite" tab)
  2. The study has been peer reviewed and published
  3. The location of each snp is included
  4. The alternate alleles are included
  5. The odds ratios or the log odds ratios are included
  6. Optional: Rs IDs
