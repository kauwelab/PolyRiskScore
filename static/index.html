What is the PRS Knowledge Base?


The purpose of the PRS Knowledge Base is to allow researchers to quickly calculate polygenic risk scores for various diseases based on published genome-wide association studies (GWAS).

The PRS Knowledge Base also facilitates the use and distribution of GWAS results by allowing researchers to upload results from their own studies to be included in future polygenic risk score calculations.

Cite the PRS Knowledge Base

The PRS Knowledge Base would not be possible without the many genome-wide association studies that have been published. We ask that if you use the PRS Knowledge Base for published work, you cite the PRS Knowledge Base, as well as all studies used in the conducted analysis.
